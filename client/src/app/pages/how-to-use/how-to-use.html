How to Use the Chat

Welcome to your personal chat assistant! Follow these quick steps to get started:

  1. Type your question or message in the input box at the bottom.
  2. Press Enter to send (or Shift + Enter for a new line).
  3. Wait for the AI to respond — it’ll appear in the chat window.
  4. Click “Suggested Prompts” in the sidebar if you need inspiration.
  5. Enjoy interactive and intelligent conversation!
